About

I began my career in the financial services industry over a decade ago, starting off in retail baking and eventually transitioning into mortgage operations and compliance. I spent much of this time using poorly designed and difficult to navigate software to complete my day-to-day responsibilities, using software that had not been implemented or configured correctly, or using software with great features that were simply not utilized.

Eventually, I had enough, and changed my focus to technology, steering my career into client success, product management, and software development roles, where I have been committed to facilitating end-user success. Leveraging my diverse background and technical profiency, I have worked as both a technical liason and a software developer. I have experience collaborating with cross-functional teams, managing stakeholder expectations, and delivering high-quality solutions, and have contributed to the design, development, and maintenance of applications improving data integrity, regulatory compliance, and workflow productivity.

For more details regarding my skills, professional background, and education, view my Experience and Portfolio, or visit my LinkedIn.
